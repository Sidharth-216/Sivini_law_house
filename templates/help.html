Step 1: Register an Account

Click "Register" from the navbar and fill in the required details to create your account.

Step 2: Login

After registering, click on "Login" in the navbar and enter your credentials to access your account.

Step 3: Profile Page

Once logged in, you will be redirected to your profile page where you can view and update your personal details.

Step 4: Booking

Navigate to the "Book" section from the navbar to schedule a consultation with a lawyer. Select your desired date and time, then confirm the booking.

Step 5: Job Apply (Client Feature)

If you're a client, click on the "Job Apply" option in the navbar, fill out the application form, and submit it to apply for job opportunities.

Step 6: Video Call

To initiate a video call, click on the "Call" option from the navbar. Choose the lawyer you want to call, and the video call interface will appear. You can start the call once the connection is made.

Step 6: Chat

To initiate a Chat, click on the "Chat" option from the navbar. Choose the lawyer you want to call, and the video call interface will appear. You can start the call once the connection is made.

Step 7: Contact Us

To contact the firm, go to the "Contact Us" page via the navbar. Fill out the form with your inquiry or request, and submit it to get in touch with the team.
